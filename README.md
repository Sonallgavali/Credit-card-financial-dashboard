# 💳 Credit Card Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing credit card transactions and customer behavior using interactive Power BI dashboards. It transforms raw financial data into actionable insights that help understand revenue trends, customer segments, and spending patterns.

The solution is divided into two dashboards:
- **Credit Card Transaction Report**
- **Credit Card Customer Report**

---

## 🎯 Objective
The objective of this project was to:
- Analyze credit card transaction data to identify revenue drivers  
- Understand customer demographics and behavior  
- Segment customers based on income, job, and education  
- Build an interactive dashboard to support business decision-making  

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
Financial institutions deal with massive amounts of transaction and customer data, but often struggle to:
- Identify which customers generate the most revenue  
- Understand spending behavior across categories  
- Track performance across time (quarters, regions, etc.)  
- Optimize customer acquisition and targeting strategies  

This project solves these challenges by creating a centralized, visual analytics dashboard.

---

## ⚙️ Approach
- Cleaned and prepared raw data in Excel  
- Created calculated fields such as:
  - Total Revenue  
  - Interest Earned  
  - Transaction Count  
- Built data models and relationships  
- Designed two dashboards in Power BI:
  - Transaction-focused analysis  
  - Customer-focused analysis  
- Added interactive elements:
  - Slicers (Quarter, Gender, Card Category, Risk Level)  
  - KPI Cards  
  - Bar Charts, Line Charts, and Distribution visuals  

---

## 📈 Results & Insights
- 💰 Total Revenue: **56.5M+**
- 💳 Highest revenue generated by **Blue card category**
- 📊 **Q4** recorded the highest transaction volume  
- 🧾 Top spending categories:
  - Bills  
  - Entertainment  
  - Fuel  
- 👨‍💼 Business professionals contribute the highest revenue segment  
- 👥 High-income group customers drive major revenue  

The dashboard enables businesses to:
- Identify high-value customers  
- Improve marketing targeting  
- Optimize customer acquisition cost  
- Make data-driven decisions  

---

## 📊 Dashboard 1: Transaction Report
Key insights include:
- Revenue and transaction trends across quarters  
- Revenue by card category (Blue, Silver, Gold, Platinum)  
- Spending behavior across expenditure types  
- Revenue contribution by customer job  
- Transaction mode analysis (Swipe, Chip, Online)  

---

## 👥 Dashboard 2: Customer Report
Key insights include:
- Revenue distribution by age group and gender  
- Customer segmentation by education and income  
- Top states contributing to revenue  
- Customer acquisition cost analysis  
- Income vs transaction behavior
